Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Viral Vibes.

What exactly is the "Revenue Engine"?+
Our complete done-for-you system covering strategy, content, editing, posting, DM automation, ads, and funnels — engineered to turn social media into a revenue machine.
Is this for influencers or service businesses?+
Exclusively for service-based businesses — coaches, consultants, agencies, clinics, and professionals. We generate clients and revenue, not just followers.
How involved do I need to be day-to-day?+
Minimal. You'll be involved during onboarding and occasional content approvals. Outside of that, you focus on delivering your service — we handle everything else.
What does "ads budget not included" mean?+
The package price covers full ad campaign management and setup. The actual spend on platforms like Meta or TikTok is paid directly to those platforms separately.
How soon will I start seeing results?+
Most clients see meaningful traction within 30–60 days. Revenue results typically compound over 60–90 days as the funnel, automation, and content build momentum.
What platforms do you manage?+
Primarily Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn. During strategy we identify where your ideal clients spend time and prioritise those platforms for max ROI.
What's the difference between the two packages?+
Growth covers the full core system. Domination adds a dedicated account manager, multi-platform expansion, weekly reports, PR outreach, A/B ad creatives, custom landing pages, and a 100K followers guarantee with full refund protection.
How do I get started?+
Click "Get Started" or message us on WhatsApp. We'll have a short discovery call, and if it's a good fit, your Revenue Engine kicks off within days.
